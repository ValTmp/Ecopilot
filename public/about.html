About EcoPilot

Leading the way to a greener future through carbon awareness

Our Mission

EcoPilot was founded with a clear mission: to empower individuals and organizations to understand and reduce their carbon footprint through accurate data and actionable insights.

We believe that by making carbon emission data accessible, understandable, and actionable, we can collectively make a significant impact on climate change. Our platform is designed to bring transparency to carbon emissions, helping users make informed decisions that lead to a more sustainable future.

The Science Behind EcoPilot

EcoPilot's calculations are based on the latest climate science and international carbon accounting standards. Our emission factors are regularly updated to reflect improvements in technology and methodologies.

For transportation emissions, we consider factors such as:

  • Fuel efficiency - Average fuel consumption rates for different vehicle types
  • Vehicle occupancy - Typical passenger loads for public transportation
  • Distance traveled - Accurate measurement of journey distances
  • Energy sources - Regional variations in electricity generation for electric vehicles
  • Vehicle age and technology - Emission differences between older and newer vehicles

Our methodology is transparent and we continually refine our algorithms based on the latest research to provide the most accurate carbon footprint calculations possible.

Open Source

EcoPilot is proud to be an open-source project. We believe that climate solutions should be accessible to everyone, and we encourage contributions from developers around the world.

Our codebase is available on GitHub, and we welcome pull requests, feature suggestions, and bug reports. By making our project open source, we aim to:

  • Ensure transparency in our calculation methodologies
  • Leverage collective expertise to improve our platform
  • Allow customization for specific regional or organizational needs
  • Foster innovation in climate tech
